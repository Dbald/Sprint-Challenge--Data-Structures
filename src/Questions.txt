1. What are the order of insertions/removals for the following data structures?

Stack:
Within a Stack, the order of insertions/removals utilizes the "last in first out (LIFO)" method.
Push is used to place it on top of the stack and Pop is used 
to remove the top value off of the stack, in LIFO order.

Queue:
Within a Queue, the order of insertions/removals utilizes the "first in first out (FIFO)" method.
Enqueue is used to add an item to the back of the queue and Dequeue is used
to remove and return an item from the front of the queue, in FIFO order.
